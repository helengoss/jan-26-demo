About

I graduated in 1995 as an Interactive Artist, the World Wide Web was just taking hold and communications is my passion, so my passion for art and technology merged with the zietgiest and I formed my company Wayoutmedia.

I have always worked with Community and Volunatary organisations, educational establishments, local governement, Museums, Libraries and with Health sector. i have studied and taught all kinds of people, from all kinds of bnackgrounds, how to utilise the Internet to make their organisations more visible, similialy I have taught people how to utilise the Internet to find out what services and cultural activities in their area. Over thirty years, through the advent of Social Media and now the dawn or Extended Realityy, I have gained a uniques perspective on how people use tech for comms and with this perspective I have designed many solutions and been involved with many research projects and international conferences. The past 15 years I have been developing my own tool for the community, the past 5 has been testing it in the field, in Novemeber 2025 I released a beta version for testin and from 2026 I have started a Full Stack Web Development course to bring the app into the world
